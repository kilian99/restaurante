The Ninth Story

We are a relaxed, vibrant neighbourhood restaurant located on Charlotte st – home to some of the best restaurants in Fitzrovia. We focus on elegantly simple French Mediterranean style cooking using top quality seasonal ingredients.

Our culture

Since we opened The Ninth in 2015 we have focused on prioritising our team and developing our culture. We believe in integrity, trust, mutual respect, teamwork and personal growth. We want every team member to find fulfilment in their work and be inspired. Our culture is the most important part of working at The Ninth.

Jun Tanaka - Chef Owner

Jun has over 30 years' experience working in London’s most iconic restaurants such as Le Gavroche, The Restaurant Marco Pierre White and The Square. He opened The Ninth in 2015 and was awarded a Michelin star in 2017. His proudest achievement to date is that The Ninth has been ranked in the Top 30 best places to work in hospitality for the past three years.

Filippo Alessandri – Head Chef

Filippo began his culinary journey at Osteria Rosso di Sera in Lake Maggiore in Italy. His first position in London was at the Michelin starred Goring hotel. He joined the team at The Ninth in 2017 as a Chef de partie then left after two years to help with the opening of Salisterra at The Upper House in Hong Kong. He came back to The Ninth as head chef in June 2022.
